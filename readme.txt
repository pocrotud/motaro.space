== Description ==

Bosa Crypto is multipurpose business theme. Bosa Crypto is beautiful, fast, lightweight, responsive, extremely customizable, multipurpose theme that you can use as a foundation to build versatile sites such as bitcoin landing, blockchain, coin, crypto, crypto trading, cryptocurrency, cryptocurrency exchange, cryptocurrency investments, digital currencies, financial template, ico, ICO Consulting, mining ethereum and preferably ideal for any type of business sites. Bosa Crypto works perfectly with Gutenberg and the most popular page builder Elementor that can easily drag-and-drop your ideas from the interface. Bosa Crypto is built with SEO, speed, and usability in mind with the multiple Header & Footer layouts, predesigned starter sites includes awesome Home & Inner Pages that is incredibly customizable and powerful enough to take a stand among the crowd. Bosa Crypto is compatible with all major plugins like WooCommerce, Yoast, Contact form 7, Mailchimp for WordPress, bbPress, etc. Looking for a Multipurpose theme? Look no further! Browse the demo to see that it's the only theme you will ever need: https://demo.bosathemes.com/bosa/crypto/

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this theme support any plugins? =

Bosa Crypto includes support for WooCommerce, Elementor, Breadcrumb NavXT and Contact From 7.
